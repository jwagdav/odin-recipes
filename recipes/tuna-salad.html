Tuna salad recipe

This is a basic staple in my house. Healthy and yummy with plenty of versatility. Enjoy this tuna salad on a sanwich or with some crackers.

  • 1 can of chunk tuna
  • 1 tbsp Mayo
  • 2 tsp yellow mustard
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 1 medium carrot
  1. Chop carrot and celery into small pieces.
  2. Add tuna, mayo, and mustard into a medium mixing bowl.
  3. Mix in with the carrot and celery.
  4. salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Enjoy on a sandwich or with crackers.
