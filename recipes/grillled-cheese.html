Grilled Cheese Recipe

This is a recipes for a basic grilled cheese with cheddar, mozzarella, and tomato if you want.

  • Sourdough bread
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tomato
  • Butter
  1. Slice cheese into thin slices.
  2. Slice tomato into thin slices.
  3. Butter the outside of your bread.
  4. Heat up a skillet to medium heat.
  5. While Skillet is heating assemble your sandwich. Add a dash of salt and pepper on top of the tomato.
  6. Cook sandwich slowly until cheese is melted and bread is browned.
