Lasagna recipe

This is a lasagna recipe which features a hearty red sauce and layers of yummy, melted cheese and aldente pasta

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 can diced tomates
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • Lean ground beef
  • Italian spice and herb mix
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  1. Dice the red onion and mince the garlic. Sautee over medium heat with 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Once the onions and garlic are soft and fragrant add the lean ground beef. Cook until browned.
  3. Once the ground beef is done add the diced tomatoes and tomate sauce.
  4. Lower to a simmer and generous spice with the Italian spice and herb mix.
  5. Boil a large pot of water over high heat with 1 tbsp of salt
  6. While the water is boiling prep your cheeses.
  7. Add lasagna noodles to the boiling water and cook until aldente. Drain immediately.
  8. In a large casserole dish add a layer of sauce to the bottom of the pan. Then Layer noodles, sauce, cheese until dish is full.
  9. Bake at 375F until cheese is bubbly and browning.
